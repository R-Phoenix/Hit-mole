How to Play

Whack the mole is an old classic game found in different game stations. This is a digital version of the otiginal game that was created. After you hit the start button, moles will pop up through the holes randomly one at a time. If you are not able to hit that mole with your hammer there and miss land the blow, you will lose one of your remaining lives. A timer countdown goes on within which you have hit the specified number of moles. You Win!!
